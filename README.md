## Commands and Options

Opening the menu:  
- Click the timer display on the right
- Esc
  
Placing flag:  
- Right CLick
- Long left click
- Activate flag mode by clicking the mine counter display on the left
  
Chord:  
- Middle Mouse button
- Left click + right click
- Double left click
  
Restarting:  
- Smiley
- R
